Features

Everything a DJ needs

One app to analyze, organize and export your entire music library.

🔬

Deep Track Analysis

Auto-detect BPM, musical key (Camelot wheel), and energy level for every track in your library. No manual tagging.

⚡

Smart Set Builder

Build DJ sets that follow a natural energy arc — warm-up, peak, cool-down — with drag-and-drop track ordering and harmonic mix suggestions.

🌊

Waveform Preview

Visual waveform rendering with cue point markers lets you preview tracks and plan transitions before you ever plug in a USB.

💿

Pioneer CDJ Export

Export your set directly to a USB formatted for Pioneer CDJ-2000NXS2, XDJ-700, XDJ-RX3 and all NXS2-compatible players.

🎛️

Traktor Support

Export your library as a Traktor NML collection, preserving cue points, BPM grids, and folder structure for seamless integration.

🌍

Bilingual Interface

Full English and Spanish support throughout the app. Switch in one click — no restart needed.

Workflow

From folder to USB in minutes

Three steps. No account. No cloud. No subscription.

01

Import

Point CueDrive at any folder on your Mac. It scans all audio files — MP3, FLAC, AAC, WAV — instantly.

02

Analyze

CueDrive detects BPM, key, and energy for each track and lets you build a set using the visual energy arc editor.

03

Export

Choose Pioneer CDJ, Traktor, or plain files. CueDrive writes everything to your USB — ready to plug into any booth.
