What it does

Everything a DJ needs,
nothing they don't.

No cloud, no subscription, no AI black box. Just fast, local analysis and smart organization.

🎵

Deep Audio Analysis

BPM, key (Camelot + standard), energy level 1–5, vocal detection, mood tags, and full track structure — all computed locally with librosa.

🎛

Smart Set Builder

Choose a template (Warm-up, Peak, Closing, Full Night) and let CueDrive build a harmonically compatible set following an energy arc — or pick your own tracks manually.

🔊

Waveform Preview

Instant waveform display with pre-computed peaks. Click any cue point marker to jump straight to the drop, break, or exit. High quality, no lag.

💿

Pioneer CDJ Export

Generates a Rekordbox-compatible SQLite database with cue points, ready to drop on a USB and plug into any CDJ-2000 or CDJ-3000.

🎚

Traktor Export

Exports a collection.nml file fully compatible with Native Instruments Traktor Pro.

🌐

EN / ES Bilingual

Full English and Spanish interface. Switch languages instantly from the sidebar — no restart needed.

Set Builder

Energy arc
templates

Every great DJ set tells a story through energy. CueDrive uses four pre-built arc templates to select and sequence tracks so the crowd goes exactly where you want them.

Warm-up 1 → 4 energy
Peak Time 3 → 5 → 3 energy
Closing 4 → 1 energy
Full Night 1 → 5 → 2 energy

Workflow

From folder to USB
in minutes.

Simple three-step workflow. No account, no internet, no cloud storage.

📂

Import

Drop in your music folder. CueDrive reads tags from MP3, FLAC, WAV, M4A, AIFF and more.

⚡

Analyze

Hit "Analyze All". BPM, key, energy, structure and cue points are computed in the background — 3 tracks at a time.

💾

Export

Build your set, preview tracks, then export to your USB as a Rekordbox database, Traktor NML, or organized files.
